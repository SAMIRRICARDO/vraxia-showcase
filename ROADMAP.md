## Not on the Roadmap

These are explicitly out of scope:

- Consumer-facing product
- General-purpose chatbot
- Replacing human decision-making
- Surveillance or monitoring of employees

---

## Strategic Bets

| Bet | Rationale |
|---|---|
| Local-first LLM for sensitive assessments | Zero cost, data sovereignty, compliance |
| pgvector over managed vector DBs | Operational simplicity, no vendor lock-in |
| Cognitive profiles as competitive moat | Not replicable, high switching cost |
| Modular agents over monolithic platform | Sell one module, expand to full OS |
