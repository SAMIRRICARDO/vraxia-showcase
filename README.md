## What is VRAXIA?

VRAXIA is a modular **AI Operating System** that unifies specialized agents across every corporate department in a single observable infrastructure.

**VRAXIA is not a chatbot.**  
**VRAXIA is not just automation.**

It is the operating layer where AI agents, organizational knowledge, and corporate memory converge — enabling enterprises to automate, scale, and permanently preserve their most critical asset: human expertise.

---

## Core Innovation — Human RAG

Traditional RAG retrieves documents.  
**Human RAG captures reasoning.**

Human RAG is VRAXIA's proprietary knowledge preservation engine. It encodes the reasoning patterns, judgment, and decision-making processes of high-performance professionals — transforming individual expertise into a permanent, queryable organizational asset.
