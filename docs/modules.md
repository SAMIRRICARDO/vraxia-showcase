# Platform Modules

VRAXIA uses a modular architecture. Each module is an independent agent cluster that can be enabled or disabled per organization.

---

## Commercial

**Function:** Sales intelligence and pipeline automation  
**Core capability:** Outreach calibration, lead qualification, pipeline stage automation  
**Human RAG integration:** Sales reasoning patterns from top performers

---

## Marketing

**Function:** Campaign orchestration and audience analysis  
**Core capability:** Content generation, campaign sequencing, audience segmentation  
**Human RAG integration:** Creative and strategic judgment from senior marketers

---

## Finance

**Function:** Financial analysis and reporting automation  
**Core capability:** Report generation, anomaly detection, cash flow projections  
**Human RAG integration:** CFO-level financial reasoning and risk judgment

---

## HR

**Function:** Talent intelligence and onboarding acceleration  
**Core capability:** Candidate evaluation, cultural fit assessment, onboarding path generation  
**Human RAG integration:** HR leadership judgment and talent pattern recognition

---

## Legal

**Function:** Contract analysis and compliance monitoring  
**Core capability:** Contract review, clause risk flagging, regulatory monitoring  
**Human RAG integration:** Senior counsel reasoning and risk tolerance patterns

---

## Operations

**Function:** Workflow orchestration and process automation  
**Core capability:** Process documentation, bottleneck detection, SOP generation  
**Human RAG integration:** Operations manager heuristics and exception handling logic

---

## Analytics

**Function:** Cross-departmental insights and KPI monitoring  
**Core capability:** Dashboard generation, trend analysis, anomaly alerts  
**Human RAG integration:** Analytical frameworks from senior data leaders

---

## Corporate Knowledge

**Function:** Expertise capture and organizational knowledge retrieval  
**Core capability:** Knowledge base indexing, expert Q&A, onboarding content generation  
**Human RAG integration:** This module IS the Human RAG layer — the backbone of all others
