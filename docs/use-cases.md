# Use Cases

VRAXIA targets medium and large enterprises in knowledge-intensive industries.

---

## Technology

**Challenge:** Senior engineers leave and take years of architectural decisions with them.

**VRAXIA application:**
- Human RAG captures reasoning patterns from principal engineers
- New engineers onboard with access to the cognitive twin of their predecessors
- Architecture decisions are queryable with context: "why was this built this way?"
- R&D intelligence: accumulated research patterns preserved and searchable

---

## Telecom

**Challenge:** Network operations knowledge is tribal — only specific engineers know why certain configurations exist.

**VRAXIA application:**
- Operations agent captures network incident resolution patterns
- Cognitive twins of senior NOC engineers guide junior staff
- Incident intelligence: past resolution reasoning available at query time

---

## Logistics

**Challenge:** Route optimization and exception handling depend on experienced dispatchers who are hard to replace.

**VRAXIA application:**
- Operations agent encodes dispatcher decision logic
- Exception handling heuristics ("when to deviate from the system") preserved
- Route and cost reasoning available to less experienced operators

---

## Engineering & Construction

**Challenge:** Project knowledge is siloed per project manager. When the PM leaves, institutional memory leaves.

**VRAXIA application:**
- Project intelligence: specification analysis patterns from senior PMs
- Technical expertise encoding: supplier evaluation logic, negotiation frameworks
- Proposal generation calibrated by the reasoning of the best estimators

---

## Healthcare

**Challenge:** Clinical protocols are documented, but the judgment of when to deviate from protocol is not.

**VRAXIA application:**
- Clinical knowledge preservation: exception logic from experienced clinicians
- Compliance automation: regulatory reasoning from legal and compliance officers
- Protocol intelligence: "when the standard protocol doesn't apply" — encoded and retrievable

---

## Financial Services

**Challenge:** Risk decisions are made by experienced analysts using heuristics that are never written down.

**VRAXIA application:**
- Risk intelligence: reasoning patterns from senior risk officers
- Regulatory knowledge: compliance reasoning from legal team encoded as cognitive chunks
- Decision traceability: every agent response linked to source reasoning patterns

---

## Industry & Manufacturing

**Challenge:** Production line expertise is held by operators with 20+ years of experience who are retiring.

**VRAXIA application:**
- Production expertise capture: machine behavior patterns, failure prediction heuristics
- Operational intelligence: maintenance decision logic from veteran engineers
- Process memory: deviation handling and quality judgment encoded before expertise is lost
